Registering for events is simple and easy:

  1. Click on the "Registration" link in the navigation menu
  2. Fill in your details including name, roll number, branch, email, and mobile number
  3. Select the events you want to participate in (you can choose multiple events)
  4. Click the "Register Now" button
  5. You will receive a confirmation message with all event details

If you face any issues, contact us through the contact page.
